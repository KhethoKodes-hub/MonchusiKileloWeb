Company Overview

Building Excellence in Mining and Construction

MONCHUSI and KILELO Mining and Enterprise is a dynamic and versatile company providing a comprehensive range of mining, industrial, and construction services in Northern Cape and beyond.

Established with the commitment to excellence and reliability, we aim to deliver high-quality solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our team of experienced professionals brings decades of industry knowledge to every project, ensuring optimal results and client satisfaction.

With our headquarters in Kathu, Northern Cape, we are strategically positioned to serve the mining and construction industries throughout the region. Our commitment to safety, quality, and sustainability sets us apart in the competitive landscape.

As a Level 1 B-BBEE Contributor with 100% Black Ownership and 51% Black Female Ownership, we are proud to contribute to economic transformation and empowerment in South Africa.

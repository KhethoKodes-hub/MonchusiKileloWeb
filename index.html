About Our Company

Building Excellence in Mining and Construction

MONCHUSI and KILELO Mining and Enterprise is a dynamic and versatile company providing a comprehensive range of mining, industrial, and construction services in Northern Cape and beyond.

Established with the commitment to excellence and reliability, we aim to deliver high-quality solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients.

As a Level 1 B-BBEE Contributor with 100% Black Ownership and 51% Black Female Ownership, we are committed to transformation and empowerment in the South African business landscape.
